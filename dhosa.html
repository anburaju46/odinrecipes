DHOSA

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup Parboiled Rice (idli-dosa rice)
  • 3/4 cup Regular Rice
  • 1/2 cup Whole Urad Dal (without skin) or Split Urad Dal (black lentils)
  • 1/4 teaspoon Fenugreek Seeds (methi dana)
  • Water, as needed
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil, for shallow frying

    • steps

    1. Soak rice, urad dal, and methi seeds.
    2. Grind the soaked ingredients into a batter.
    3. Ferment the batter for 12-24 hours
    4. Heat a non-stick dosa pan.
    5. Grease the pan with oil or ghee.
    6. Pour the batter and spread evenly.
    7. Cook the dosa for 1-2 minutes.
    8. Flip and cook the other side.
    9. Serve hot with your favorite filling.
