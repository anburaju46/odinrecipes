BLACK TEA

Ingredients

  • 1. Tea leaves (Camellia sinensis)
  • 2. Water
  • 3. Sugar
  • 4. Milk or creamer
  • 6. Spices (cinnamon, ginger, cardamom)

steps

1. Boil water
2. Add tea leaves (1 tsp per cup)
3. Steep for 3-5 minutes
4. Strain and add milk/sugar (optional)
5. Serve and enjoy!
